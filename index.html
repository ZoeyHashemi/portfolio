About Me

I’m Zoey a Passionate Interactive Media Design Student, Located in Ottawa, Canada.

As a UX designer, I believe that design should be user-centric, intuitive, and engaging. I possess a solid foundation in human-centered design, along with proficiency in industry-standard tools such as Adobe Creative Suite and HTML/CSS. I am eager to collaborate with a team of designers and developers on projects that have a meaningful impact on users. With my quick learning ability and problem-solving skills, I am ready to take on new challenges and contribute to your organization’s success as a co-op user experience designer.

I love the art of storytelling and applying it to my work. Although I may not have the power to solve major global issues, I do have the ability to make a positive impact by improving the experience for users in various areas like job performance, buying processes, or banking. I believe that through storytelling and creative thinking, I can help your business improve and achieve success. Let’s work together to create a better user experience and tell a great story for your customers.
